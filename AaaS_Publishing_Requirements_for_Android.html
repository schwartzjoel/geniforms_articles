AaaS Publishing Requirements for Android

Developer Account

Google charges a one time $25 fee to get a developer account on Google Play, which lets you publish Android apps. 

It is your responsibility to pay for and maintain your Developer Account subscription.

Please see this article on how to create a developer account with Google.


Privacy Policy

As part of this process, you will also need to provide a link to your company's Privacy Policy.
This must be hosted as a page on your company's website.
Please see this article if you don't already have a Privacy Policy page.


Display Images 

To capture screenshots:

  • Press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons at the same time. 
  • You’ll see an animation on the screen, indicating that the screenshot was saved. 
Android will save the screenshot to your Gallery. Launch the Gallery app and tap Screenshots to view and share your screenshots. 


For other devices, search the Google Play Store for screenshot apps that apply for your device. 


NOTE: Images must be provided with the exact dimensions stated. Images must be JPEG or PNG (no transparency).
