Adding an Email Connector


Now that you've successfully designed and tested a form and customized a PDF output, it's time to push your data to an external system.


In the above video, you'll be taken through how to add and configure an Email Connector for a form - triggered upon form upload. Sending out an email(s) to static or dynamic addresses containing copies of form-captured data in the format of your choosing.


This is just the start of pushing your data out to external systems, as we have quite a few pre-built Form Connectors at your disposal.
