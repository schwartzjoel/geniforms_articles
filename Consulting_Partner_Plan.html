Consulting Partner Plan

We’ve identified a type of customer who falls within the ‘specialist consultant’ category and therefore assembled a plan that caters to their specific platform requirements.


These specialist consultants differ from a typical reseller in that they don’t want the responsibility for their clients' technical support and billing. They need a platform to create mobile solutions and seamlessly share them among their various customer accounts. 


Hence Your Companys’ Consulting Partner Plan was born.


To gain full access to an exclusive Consulting Partner Account, you’ll need to complete a short assessment to ensure you’re technically capable on the Your Company platform and can deliver solutions to your clients.


When you’ve successfully passed the assessment, your account will be upgraded. You’ll enjoy Consulting Partner privileges, including a certification badge you can use to flaunt your qualification as a Certified Your Company Consulting Partner.

Who can become an Your Company Consulting Partner?

Anybody with a bit of technical savvy looking to get into the mobile app market to provide value-added services to clients they introduce to Your Company is welcome to join the program.


Your Company will take care of your clients' support, billing, and training so you can focus on things like building new forms, scoping new solutions, and managing accounts and data.

How it Works

  • Free master consulting partner account with the ability to create and associate client accounts.
  • 15% recurring revenue share on consulting clients brought to Your Company.
  • Billing is handled by us, minimizing admin.
  • A certificate and badge for the consultant’s website.
  • Ongoing support via a dedicated account manager.
  • 10 free premium demo/support user licenses.
  • Clients & Repository menu area access.


When upgraded to a Consulting Partner, your organization account is effectively upgraded to a Master level account.


The Master account is the top-level account you can use as your testing sandbox and a central area for demos and evaluations. If you intend on selling ready-made app packages to your clients, then your Master account is where you can create such content.


You can have up to 10 users on this Master account free of charge for demonstration and testing purposes.


One key function that you gain from a Master account is the ability to create separate organization accounts for each of your clients. These client accounts are completely standalone - each has its own set of Users, Screens, Data Sources, Tasks, etc.


Client accounts are linked to your Master account, and your revenue share as a Consulting Partner for that client is calculated from the number of clients you have actively linked to your Consulting Partner account.

Demo & Support Users

All Your Company Consulting Partner accounts receive 10 free users for development, testing, and demo purposes.


A non-billable support user in each associated client account is permitted, provided the support user's email address matches an email address of a user created in your master account.
