Branding Center

The Branding Center is a single area in the platform for you to manage your App as a Service add-ons, all the way from colours and imagery, to app store listing text and publishing.


This area also incorporates our standard process of reviewing, optimising and quality-checking every branded app submission, as we strive to ensure the best possible app result for all involved.


Also included are all the existing options for Website as a Service (if you have subscribed to this), giving you one place to manage all aspects of your white-label solution.


Navigate the side menu Platform&Billing > Branding Center which will take you to the Branding Center home page.



The Branding Center is the single area in the platform for you to manage your App as a Service Apps covering:


App Settings

This area allows for the upload of custom icons as well as enabling the option to enable/disable the 'Forgot Password' button in-app allowing users to access the change password workflow directly from the application.


App Branding

This area is mainly there to set up the cosmetics of the login screen of your app from the button colors to the background colors and splash art. You can also upload a custom icon for your application here.


Review and Submission

This area also incorporates our standard process of reviewing, optimizing, and quality-checking every branded app submission, as we strive to ensure the best possible app result for all involved.



This series of articles focuses on App as a Service. However, information to help you manage your Website as a Service can find at Website as a Service (Your Own Branded Web Portal).
