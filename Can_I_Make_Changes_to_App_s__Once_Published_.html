Can I Make Changes to App(s) Once Published?

Once your app has been published to the relevant store(s), should you wish to make a change - be it anything from changing the name/description to changing imagery - you can update this into the Branding Center.

We will then have to create a new app version which will then be required to go through the App Review Process once again.

This applies to both iOS and Android apps - the only difference being that the Google Play review process takes approx. 8 hours to release the new version, whereas Apple takes 7-10 days.

Your current app(s) however, will remain functional in the stores till the new ones have been approved.


Note: when our developers attempt to log in to your Google developer account, as part of their security process, Google will notify you of this activity.
To avoid these notifications going forward, please add Wrens (USA) to your list of approved devices within your Google developer account.
