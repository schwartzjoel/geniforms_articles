App Store Distribution: Adding Our App Manager to Your Developer Account

Due to new two factor authorisation (2FA) requirements by Apple, we cannot log with your main Apple Developer account credentials to maintain your branded app. This is because we cannot easily receive the SMS verification code that forms part of the 2FA login process.


In order to maintain your branded app and provide new app updates, our App Manager (appnmanager@gmail.com) Apple ID needs to be linked with your developer account.


We need you to create this linkage (as we can’t log into your 2FA account) before we are able to publish any app updates for your iOS app.


Please make sure you have the login details handy for your Apple Developer account, then follow these steps:


1.  Log into the Apple Developer portal at https://developer.apple.com


2.  Click on “People”

3.  Click on “Go to App Store Connect”, then click the + icon

4.  A modal window to create a New User will appear.
Fill it in with the following details:

  • First Name: App
  • Last Name: Manager
  • Email: appnmanager@gmail.com
  • Roles: Tick all possible - Admin, Developer and App Manager must be ticked.
  • Developer Resources: Tick “Access to Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles”. 

Then click on "Invite"

6.  That’s it! 
We’ll get your invite and activate our inclusion in your Developer account.
