Adding a DropBox Connection


Dropbox is a cloud storage service that lets you save and sync files online to your devices. You can use Dropbox links to share files and folders with other people without sending large attachments.


Our Dropbox Connectors will allow you to push and pull data from a Dropbox database. So if you’re using the Dropbox platform, you’ll soon enjoy smoother data transfer to and from Your Company.

To add a Dropbox Connection, navigate to the Side Menu > Connected Data > Connections.

More information on how to add a Connection can be found here, Adding Data Connections.


On the Connections page

  • Click the ADD CONNECTION button on the top right to bring up a list of connections.
  • Click on the Dropbox option.


Now click the Authorize button to see a pop-up window to sign in with your Dropbox credentials.



Once you have signed in to your dropbox account, the connection will be verified automatically -  you'll see a message indicating that you are now connected on the right-hand side.



Next, click the "SAVE" button on the top right-hand side of the page, and now your Dropbox account is linked to Apppenate.
