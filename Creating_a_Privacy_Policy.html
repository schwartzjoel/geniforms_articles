Creating a Privacy Policy

A web-hosted privacy policy page is required as part of the branded app publishing process, as well as by several integrations when you setup a branded website (i.e. Website as a Service).


If you don't already have a Privacy Policy, you will need to create one and host this on your website as a permanent link.
You can generate a privacy policy document quite easily thanks to policy generator tools - just do a Google search for "privacy policy generator".Â 


Here's one such site that generates a reasonably standard privacy policy:

https://app-privacy-policy-generator.firebaseapp.com

Once you have appropriate privacy policy words generated, add this as a web page to your standard company website.


The result should be that your policy can be accessed via a website link - e.g. www.mycompany.com/privacy-policy.
Make a note of this link and use it when prompted in your branded app/website process.


If you're not sure about how to add a Privacy Policy page to your company website, then ask the IT person/provider that maintains your website for help.


Please note that your Privacy Policy may have legal implications for your business.
If in doubt, consult your legal advisors to ensure that your policy document meets your needs.
