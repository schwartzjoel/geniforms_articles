Adding Client Accounts

Consulting Partner (CP) accounts have the ability to create, and associate client accounts brought to Your Company. 


In a master CP account, clients brought to Your Company that you're a consultant for can be added in two ways. A new account can be created, or an existing account can be added that displays on your Clients page.


Navigate to Platform & Billing > Clients to view all associated client accounts, add new or existing, view audits, and switch account contexts, jumping into your client's account to manage their apps and data.


Creating a New Associated Account

On the Clients page, hit the Add New + button to create a new client account associated with your master CP account. On the Create Client page, you'll have basic, regional, user, and device options to configure before creating the new client.



After creating, your context will switch to the newly created account, and the client's users will need to be created and invited to use your apps and solutions.


More info on context switching and managing account content might be of interest.


By default, when creating a new client, a support user is automatically created in the client's account to provide additional services.

A non-billable user using the user's email address who created the account.

Adding an Existing Account

On the Clients page, hit the Add Existing button to add an existing client as one of your clients. You'll need to contact the Organization first to retrieve the information required, Company Id, and Private Token.


Your new client can locate this information in the Integration Settings of their Organization Setup.
